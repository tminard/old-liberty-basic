Name of Program:        DEANSLIB.DLL
Author of Program:      Dean Hodgson (with a contribution by Alyce Watson)
Email:                  dhodgson@nexus.edu.au
Release date:           24 June, 1998
Description:

DEANSLIB is a DLL that adds over 100 commands and functions to Liberty
Basic via CALLDLLs. The functions range include: disk file & networking
commands, font assignments (including italic, undelined & bold), printout
functions, mouse support, date and time setting & reading, maths functions,
simple PC speaker sound, special Windows functions, and a raft of functions
to support editbox, listbox and comboboxes. This library replaces the
earlier DEANSDLL.
